Hot Water Recipe

Making Hot Water

If you are wondering "What is the secret to making Hot Water?" Well, you have come to the right place.

Below, we shall deliniate the material and skill required to make you a master at preparing Hot Water. Good Luck.

Ingrediants

  • Coffee Mug
  • Water
  • Kettle

Steps

  1. Fill the Kettle with Water
  2. Plug in the Kettle
  3. Turn on the Kettle
  4. Pour the now Hot Water into your mug
  5. Enjoy your Hot Water!
