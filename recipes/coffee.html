Coffee Recipe

Making Coffee

If you are wondering "What is the secret to making instant Coffee?" Well, you have come to the right place.

Below, we shall deliniate the material and skill required to make you a master at preparing instant coffee. Good Luck.

Ingrediants

  • Coffee Mug
  • Spoon
  • Instant Coffee
  • Hot Water
  • Milk (optional)
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Whiskey (optional)

Steps

  1. Stand your Coffee Mug upright
  2. Place (using a spoon) the desired amount of coffee in your cup
  3. Pour Hot Water into your cup
  4. Mix it with a spoon
  5. (Optional) Add the desired supplement listed above
  6. Enjoy your Coffee!
