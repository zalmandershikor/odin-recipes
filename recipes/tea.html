Tea Recipe

Making Tea

If you are wondering "What is the secret to making Hot Tea?" Well, you have come to the right place.

Below, we shall deliniate the material and skill required to make you a master at preparing Hot Tea. Good Luck.

Ingrediants

  • Tea Mug
  • Spoon
  • Tea Bag
  • Hot Water

Steps

  1. Stand your Tea Mug upright
  2. Place the Tea Bag in the Mug
  3. Pour Hot Water in the Mug
  4. Stir the tea
  5. Enjoy your Tea
